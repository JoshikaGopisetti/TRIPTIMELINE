About Smoky Mountains

Location: United States, North Carolina and Tennessee

Climate: Temperate climate with four distinct seasons. Summers are warm and humid, while winters can be cold with occasional snowfall.

Attractions

1. Clingmans Dome: The highest point in the Smoky Mountains, offering panoramic views.

2. Cades Cove: A beautiful valley with historic buildings, wildlife viewing, and scenic drives.
