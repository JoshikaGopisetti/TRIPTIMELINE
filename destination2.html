About Rocky Mountains

Location: United States and Canada, stretching from Colorado to British Columbia.

Climate: Varied climate with cold winters and mild summers. Higher elevations experience significant snowfall and cooler temperatures year-round.

Attractions

1. Rocky Mountain National Park: Known for its high-altitude trails, wildlife, and breathtaking scenery.

2. Banff National Park: Located in Alberta, Canada, offering stunning lakes, mountains, and hot springs.
